Welcome to America

Real-World English Simulations

Interactive Scenarios

Practice English in Real Situations

Immerse yourself in common American environments. Each scenario is designed for guided, interactive practice—not just vocabulary. Activity completion and progress are tracked in Moodle.

Airport Arrival & Customs

Immigration, baggage claim, and airport announcements. Practice what to say when you land.

Uber or Taxi Ride

Book a ride, confirm pickup, and communicate with the driver. Common phrases and directions.

Subway & Public Transportation

Buy tickets, ask for routes, and navigate buses and trains.

Hotel Check-in

Reception, room requests, amenities, and checkout. Professional hotel communication.

Restaurant Ordering

Order food, handle dietary needs, ask for the bill, and tip appropriately.

Asking for Directions

Ask for and give directions. Streets, landmarks, and navigation vocabulary.

How It Works

Each scenario is delivered as an interactive module in Moodle. You'll hear realistic dialogue, respond to situations, and receive feedback. Your participation and completion are recorded for progress tracking and reporting.
